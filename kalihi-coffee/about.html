Who We Are

Kalihi Coffee wants to share the goodness of coffee and the joy of community with the people of Kalihi!

We source our beans from Honolulu Coffee Company and partner with local vendors to create delicious flavors for our drinks.

What We Value

  • Supporting Local — All our syrups and coffee beans are sourced from Hawai'i companies
  • Giving Back — We donate 10% of our earnings to local non-profits
  • Community — Check out our Instagram for current events
