Welcome to Kalihi Coffee

Who We Are

We are a small business located in the Kalihi neighborhood of Honolulu, HI.

Our team is passionate about roasting good coffee, creating space to gather with others, and giving back to the local community.

Featured Drinks

  • Vanilla Latte — Steamed milk, espresso, and house-made vanilla syrup
  • Strawberry Matcha Latte — Japanese Matcha with fresh strawberry sauce and oat milk, served over ice
  • Drip Coffee — Locally grown Kona coffee, dark roast
